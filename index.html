📡 Monitor Markets

Kalshi Signal continuously scans Kalshi — a CFTC-regulated prediction market exchange — for contracts with significant probability movements, identifying events that are actively repricing in real time.

🔍 Research Events

When a significant move is detected, the system researches the underlying news event driving the probability shift, synthesizing context from multiple sources into a concise, accurate narrative.

🎬 Publish Videos

A short-form analysis video is generated and published directly to TikTok, delivering timely prediction market insights to viewers in an accessible, engaging format.
